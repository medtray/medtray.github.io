ABOUT

I am a Machine Learning Researcher at Nokia Bell Labs. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from Lehigh University, USA, in 2022. I obtained my Master of Science in Computer Science from University of Louisville, USA, in 2018. I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from Tunisia Polytechnic School, Tunisia, in 2016. My research areas include Machine learning, Information Retrieval, and Natural Language Processing. Here is my CV.
